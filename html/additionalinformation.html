do it yourself
watch this video to learn how to paint your own custom books of mormon! below the video, there are additional skills you might consider learning.

  1. Layering Techniques
    • Glazing: Applying thin layers of translucent paint to create depth and richness of color.
    • Scumbling: Using a dry brush to apply a thin, opaque layer of paint over a base layer, creating texture and visual interest.
    • Impasto: Applying thick layers of paint with a palette knife or brush to create a textured surface.
  2. Texturing Techniques
    • Sgraffito: Scratching into wet paint to reveal the layers underneath, adding texture and detail.
    • Stippling: Dabbing or stippling paint onto the canvas with a brush or sponge to create a textured surface
    • Collage: Incorporating various materials such as paper, fabric, or found objects into the painting to add dimension and texture.
  3. Blending Techniques
    • Wet-on-Wet: Applying wet paint onto a wet surface to blend colors seamlessly and create smooth transitions.
    • Dry Brushing: Using a dry brush with minimal paint to create a subtle blending effect and add texture.
    • Feathering: Softening the edges of painted areas by gently blending with a dry brush or feather-like strokes, creating a soft and gradual transition between colors.
